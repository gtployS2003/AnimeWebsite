Naruto is a boy ostracized because he has the Nine-Tailed Fox sealed within him, but he is determined to become Hokage, the leader of the Hidden Leaf Village. Along the way, he makes friends and enemies, facing challenges that test his abilities and beliefs.
Humanity vs. Titans
Attack on Titan
In a world where humanity must live within walls to protect themselves from giant Titans, Eren and his friends join the Survey Corps after the walls are breached. They fight for survival and uncover terrifying secrets about the origin of the Titans and humanity's history.
Pirate Adventure
One Piece
Luffy dreams of becoming the Pirate King. He and the Straw Hat crew embark on a journey across the Grand Line to find the One Piece, the legendary treasure left behind by the former Pirate King. Their adventure is filled with danger, battles, and deep bonds of friendship.
Heroes in a Superpowered World
My Hero Academia
Born without superpowers in a world where most people have them, Deku remains determined to become a great hero. After inheriting the "One For All" power, he enrolls in UA High School, training hard to protect those he loves and fight against villains.
