Plot Summary

In a world overrun by giant, man-eating Titans, humanity's last hope lies behind massive walls. Eren Yeager joins the Survey Corps to fight the Titans after witnessing the destruction of his hometown. As he battles, he uncovers dark secrets about the Titans and the true history of humanity. The series explores themes of survival, sacrifice, and the nature of freedom.
