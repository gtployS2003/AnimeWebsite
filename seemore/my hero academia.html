Follow Deku and his classmates as they train to become the next generation of heroes. Witness their battles against powerful villains and personal struggles.

Plot Summary

Far far away, In a world where almost everyone has superpowers, or "Quirks," Izuku Midoriya dreams of becoming a hero despite being born without one. After inheriting the powerful Quirk "One For All," he enrolls in U.A. High School to train as a hero. Alongside his classmates, he faces villains and learns what it truly means to be a hero. The series highlights the challenges of heroism, courage, and personal growth.

Main Characters

Meet Deku, All Might, Bakugo, and the other aspiring heroes who train at U.A. High School.

Izuku Midoriya

A determined and compassionate young hero who inherits the powerful Quirk "One For All" and strives to become the world's greatest hero.

Katsuki Bakugo

A fiercely competitive and hot-headed hero-in-training with the explosive Quirk "Explosion," driven by his desire to surpass all others.

Ochaco Uraraka

A kind-hearted and optimistic hero with the Quirk "Zero Gravity," who dreams of becoming a hero to support her family financially.

Tenya Ida

A disciplined and responsible hero-in-training with the Quirk "Engine," who is dedicated to upholding justice and leading by example.

Shoto Todoroki

A reserved and powerful hero with the dual Quirks "Half-Cold Half-Hot," struggling to reconcile his complicated family past and forge his own path.

Eijiro Kirishima

AA courageous and loyal hero with the Quirk "Hardening," known for his strong sense of manliness and unwavering determination.

Tsuyu Asui

A calm and level-headed hero with the frog-like Quirk "Frog," valued for her practical thinking and adaptability in tough situations.

Momo Yaoyorozu

A cyborg and the Straw Hat Pirates’ shipwright, known for his impressive mechanical skills and dream of building and sailing a ship that can reach the end of the Grand Line.
